About UDGAMA

The premier zonal student congress of IEEE Guntur Sub Section.

Empowering the Next Generation of Engineers

UDGAMA 2026 is the flagship annual event orchestrated by the IEEE Guntur Sub Section. Designed as a melting pot of ideas, innovation, and industry-academic interaction, UDGAMA serves as a critical platform for aspiring engineers across the region.

Our mission is to foster a culture of technical excellence. Through a series of rigorously curated technical symposiums, hands-on workshops, and competitive events, we aim to equip students with the practical skills and theoretical knowledge required to thrive in modern technological landscapes.

Whether you are a researcher looking to present your findings, a coder ready for a hackathon, or a student seeking inspiration from industry veterans, UDGAMA 2026 is your gateway to the future.
